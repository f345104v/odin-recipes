chorizo Street Tacos

its 3 a.m. and the club just let out and we drunk whats the next step

well thats to walk to tacos el chuy 2 blocks down the club to sober up

OR go home and make them yourself just dont drink and drive

ingredients

  • chorizo links
  • peppers
  • tortillas
  • onions
  • cilantro

steps

  1. crumble chorizo links and cook with onion and peppers on skillet
  2. heat up tortillas on same skillet you cooked the chorizo
  3. serve chorizo on tortilla and add chopped fresh onions and cilantro
  4. add topping to your liking and enjoy
